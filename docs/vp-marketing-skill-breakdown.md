# VP Marketing Skill Breakdown

Coding agents give GTM teams superpowers by turning repeatable marketing, sales, and ops work into source-backed workflows that can be reviewed, improved, and reused.

Use this overview when explaining the repo to GTM leaders who care less about agent mechanics and more about business outcomes.

## Marketing

- **Campaign Generator:** turns campaign briefs, notes, and source docs into a complete outbound campaign package with strategy checkpoint, email sequence, LinkedIn copy, subject lines, and open questions.
- **Brand Guidelines Checker:** helps marketers and non-designers review creative ideas or assets against brand guidelines before they go live.
- **HTML Mockup Builder:** turns a page or campaign brief into a reviewable web mockup so teams can align faster before design or engineering work begins.

## Sales And Revenue

- **Sales Sequence Builder:** creates personalized outbound, follow-up, re-engagement, and signal-based sales sequences from account context and campaign goals.
- **Named Account Trigger Radar:** ranks target accounts by why-now signals and recommends the next best seller action.
- **AI Audit Account Report:** builds account-specific AI opportunity reports from product usage, adoption gaps, and rep-ready messaging.

## Marketing Ops And GTM Ops

- **Inbound Lead Audit + CX Map:** reviews what happened after a lead raised their hand and maps the customer experience from form fill to follow-up.
- **No Lead Left Behind Audit:** flags missed, slow, or low-quality lead follow-up and creates owner-ready actions.
- **Find Lead Account Owner:** resolves the right owner for a lead or account using a clear CRM fallback path.
- **Daily Lead Steward:** creates a daily operating view of high-intent leads, priority accounts, blockers, and recommended next actions.
- **Daily Notable Lead Audit:** reviews new high-intent leads each day and tracks whether they are moving toward meetings.
- **Daily SDR Unconverted Hand-Raiser List:** gives SDRs a daily prioritized list of hand-raisers who have not converted yet.
- **Weekly No Lead Left Behind Rollup:** summarizes weekly lead follow-up patterns, gaps, and operational fixes.
- **Friday Unconverted MQL Sync:** gives the team a weekly check on unconverted MQLs and follow-up status.
- **No Lead Left Behind Standup Brief:** produces a concise standup update focused on movement, blockers, and owner actions.

## Content Ops And Social

- **Company Recording To Postable Clip:** finds the right moment in a meeting or recording, creates a short clip, and routes it for approval.
- **Clip Request Intake:** collects the source recording, target quote, approval owner, and intended destination for a clip.
- **Clip Approval And Handoff:** routes finished clips for review, logs assets, and creates distribution handoff tasks.

## Customer-Friendly Framing

For customer or executive conversations, lead with the business workflows:

- Campaign acceleration
- Lead follow-up QA
- Account prioritization
- Sales personalization
- Content repurposing

Then explain that Zapier MCP and SDK give coding agents a safe way to reach the systems where those workflows already happen.
