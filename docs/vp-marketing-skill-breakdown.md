# GTM Skill Breakdown

Coding agents help GTM teams turn scattered context into useful work products: campaign plans, retros, customer proof, lead audits, account briefs, sales sequences, and content handoffs.

Use this overview with marketing, sales, customer operations, business operations, and IT partners who need to map concrete use cases before connecting systems.

| Skill | Use when / problem it solves | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mega Campaign Generator | A team has a campaign idea, source docs, audience notes, and scattered discussion, but no clean launch package. | Builds a review-ready campaign package with positioning, audience, message angles, email/LinkedIn drafts, assumptions, and open questions. |
| Campaign Postmortem | A campaign ended, missed target, or created learnings that are stuck in meetings and anecdotes. | Pulls together goals, timeline, performance, team feedback, and source notes into a concise retro: what happened, what worked, what broke, and what changes next. |
| Brand Guidelines Builder | Marketers need a fast first-pass review of copy, designs, or ideas against brand rules. | Turns brand guidance into a practical checklist and flags likely issues before creative goes to final review. |
| HTML Mockup Builder | A team needs to see a page, landing page, or campaign concept before asking design or engineering for help. | Creates a simple reviewable HTML mockup from a brief so stakeholders can react to the actual experience. |
| Find Customer Social Proof | A marketer or seller needs the right customer example, quote, stat, slide, or peer story for a campaign, page, deck, or prospect conversation. | Searches approved customer-proof sources, ranks the best matches, and returns usage notes so the team knows what can be used where. |
| Customer Story Writer | A team has an interview transcript, notes, or proof points and needs a story draft that is specific, sourced, and usable. | Turns approved customer material into either a narrative blog/campaign story or a metric-forward customer story page, with quotes, claims, and approval gaps called out. |
| Sales Sequence Builder | A seller needs relevant outreach but only has rough account context, a trigger, and a CTA. | Drafts a short, source-backed sequence with subject lines, rep notes, assumptions, and gaps for review. |
| Customer Deck Builder | A seller or customer success lead needs a customer-facing deck for discovery, QBR, renewal, expansion, onboarding, or follow-up. | Turns approved account context, usage signals, meeting goals, and use-case guidance into a structured deck and generates it in Gamma through Zapier MCP. |
| Named Account Trigger Radar | Sales or ABM needs to know which target accounts deserve attention now. | Ranks accounts by actionability, explains the why-now evidence, and recommends the next owner action. |
| AI Audit Account Report | A rep needs a practical account brief for an AI adoption or expansion conversation. | Builds an account-level report from approved usage and account context, including likely opportunities, gaps, and talk tracks. |
| Cross-CRM Opportunity Sync | A company has multiple CRMs and deals, accounts, or customer records can get split across systems. | Keeps opportunity and account context aligned across CRMs, flags conflicts, preserves ownership and attribution, and creates a reviewable audit trail. |
| Support Ticket Triage Agent | Support reps spend too much time researching each ticket across the helpdesk, bug tracker, backlog, and docs. | Pulls ticket context, searches the right systems in parallel, classifies the issue, and gives the rep a clear next action. |
| MCP Urgent Caller Triage For Law Firms | A law firm needs to know which inbound callers need attention first before returning calls. | Reads call transcripts, classifies urgency, matches callers to approved case context, and prepares a prioritized callback list for human review. |
| Inbound Lead Audit + CX Map | A leader wants to understand what happened after a high-intent lead raised their hand. | Reconstructs the lead journey from form fill to follow-up, showing what worked, what failed, and where the experience needs fixing. |
| No Lead Left Behind Lead Treatment Audit | Marketing Ops or SDR leadership needs to catch missed, slow, or low-quality follow-up. | Audits lead handling, scores issues, resolves owners, and turns the findings into clear follow-up actions. |
| Find Lead Account Owner | A lead needs routing and the owner is unclear across contact, company, and deal records. | Finds the best owner using a strict fallback path and explains which source decided it. |
| Daily Lead Steward | Managers need a daily operating view of high-intent leads that need attention. | Produces a watchlist of priority leads, blockers, meeting movement, and recommended actions. |
| Company Recording To Postable Clip | A team knows a useful moment happened in a meeting or event recording but does not have a clean clip. | Finds the source recording, locates the moment, cuts a short clip when permitted, and routes it for approval. |
| Daily Notable Lead Audit | The team needs a daily check on new high-intent leads and whether they are moving. | Reviews new leads, highlights gaps and owner actions, and tracks meeting progress. |
| Daily Unconverted Hand-Raisers - SDR Call List | SDRs need a focused daily list of hand-raisers who have not converted. | Produces a prioritized call list with the context SDRs need to act. |
| Weekly No Lead Left Behind Unconverted MQL Analysis Rollup | Leaders need to see weekly patterns in unconverted MQL follow-up. | Summarizes unresolved leads, follow-up gaps, and process fixes. |
| Friday Unconverted MQL Audit Sync | The team needs a weekly closeout view before the next operating cycle. | Creates a concise Friday status view of unconverted MQLs, accountability, and blockers. |
| No Lead Left Behind Standup Brief | A meeting needs a short update on movement and blockers, not a long lead dump. | Produces a brief standup summary with the key changes, risks, and actions. |
| Clip Request Intake | Content or social needs a clean way to request a clip. | Captures source recording, target moment, approval owner, and destination. |
| Clip Approval And Handoff | A clip is ready but still needs review and publishing coordination. | Routes the clip for approval, logs the asset, and creates the distribution handoff. |
