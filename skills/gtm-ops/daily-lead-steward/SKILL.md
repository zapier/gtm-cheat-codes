# Daily Lead Steward

Use this skill to keep a persistent operating view of high-intent leads, especially hand-raisers and enterprise or large-enterprise MQLs.

## Workflow

1. Load the current lead tracker and live CRM state.
2. Identify leads with no meeting, stale follow-up, unclear ownership, or high-value routing concerns.
3. Recommend next actions without acting autonomously.
4. Generate a reviewable dashboard or digest.
5. Update persistent state only after QA.

## Guardrails

- Recommendation-only by default.
- Do not auto-send rep messages.
- Keep the source-of-truth tracker private.
