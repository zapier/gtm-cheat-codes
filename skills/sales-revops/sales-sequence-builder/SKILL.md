# Sales Sequence Builder

Use this skill when a GTM team needs rep-ready sequence copy for outbound, event follow-up, re-engagement, or signal-based outreach.

## Workflow

1. Gather the minimum context needed to make the sequence specific.
2. Separate reusable elements from reference copy from elements that must change for this audience.
3. Draft concise steps with clear CTAs and plainspoken language.
4. Add operator notes for personalization, claims, and routing.
5. Keep sensitive signals useful without sounding invasive.

## Guardrails

- Do not blindly port copy from another campaign.
- Do not imply unsupported claims, custom audits, or executive involvement.
- Do not create Gmail or CRM drafts without approval.
